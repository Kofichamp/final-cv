Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a bit hit at parties. My father recommends:

"Make this the night before so that flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temperature before you serve it. In the winter, try serving it warm."

Ingredient

  • 1 8Oz. jar sundried tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 c. kalamata olives
  • 1 t. capers

Instruction

  1. Combine tomaoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend until as smooth as possible.
  2. Add carpers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times unti they are incorporated, but still retain some.
  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and basil garnish (optional).
